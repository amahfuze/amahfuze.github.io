About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for Space Trooper on Android devices. Our game is designed to be safe and fun for players of all ages, including children.

Important: Space Trooper is a completely offline game. We do not collect any personal information from players. The game can be played entirely without an internet connection.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Space Trooper is designed to work completely offline without any network connection required.

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • No personal information of any kind
  • No device identifiers or tracking data
  • No game progress data sent to servers
  • No crash reports or analytics
  • No location data
  • No names, addresses, or contact information
  • No photos, videos, or camera access
  • No contact lists or personal messages
  • No social media information

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, there is no data usage to describe. Your game progress and settings are stored only on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

Children Under 13

Since we do not collect any information, there are no privacy concerns for children. The game is completely safe for children to play offline.

For Parents:

  • No account creation or registration needed
